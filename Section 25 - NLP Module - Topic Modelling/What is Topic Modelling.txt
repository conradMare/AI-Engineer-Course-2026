Topic modelling:

"Takes a set of documents ( individual pieces of text -> Rows of text in a DataFrame or items in a list )"

Topic modelling will take the documents and scan it for patterns,
We can then group similar documents together ito topics.

TOPIC MODELLING IS AN EXAMPLE OF UNSUPERVISED LEARNING.

We don't need any labels in out documents to find topics,
the algorythms work by identifying similar word patterns throughout the documents,
from there the algorythm works to identify what each document is talking about,
oce the algorythm can do this they can group documents together easily.
