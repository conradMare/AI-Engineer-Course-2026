Natural Language Processing (NLP)

Def: "A field of AI that allows computers to understand, interpret and generate human language data"

Techniques used to process and analyze human language data:
1 - Statistics
2 - Machine Learning (ML)
3 - Deep Learning (DL)

By leveraging NLP techniques you will be able to:
1 - Save massive amounts of time when working with text data,
2 - Uncover insights that previously might not have been uncovered
