Numerical Representation of Text:

Getting data in the right format for machine learning (Text Pre-processing) -> Where Text Vectorization is involved.

Passing clean text into an algorythm is not enough, need to convert the text into a numeric representation that the machine algorythm can understand.

There are 2 ways to do this:
1 - The Bag of Words models,
2 - Term Frequence Inverse Document Frequency (TI IDF)

Bag of Words Model:
"Counts which words appear in which documents."
(Simple and easy to understand, but you do lose a lot of context.)

Term Frequency Inverse Document Frequency (TF IDF):
"Calculates the importance of that word for that particular document."
"Takes onto account how that word appears in each of the other documents."
In short, it gives a bit more context.
